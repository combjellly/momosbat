RECEPTION

Time to celebrate (i.e. party!). After the service, head to the main lobby where hot hors d'oeuvres will be served. There will also be a sushi/sashimi bar for everybody and a martini bar for adults and pina coladas for kids. We paid a flat fee for these so don't hold back on our account. In fact, don't hold back all night. We want you to have fun.

Then, everyone will head to the auditorium on the upper level. Look for your place card near the entrance. There will be a sit-down dinner and lots of dancing. Our DJ will be Superfly. They are lots of fun and the dancing Fly Girls will keep you moving.

At some point, a bunch of you will hoist me up on a chair and dance me around. I have no idea why. Be gentle.


Nicholas from Party Impressions will have cool party extras happening in the lobby. And there will be giveaways and prizes etc. etc. etc. And speeches. Short speeches. And a candle-lighting ceremony which I still have to figure out.

At about 11:30, there will be a sweet table. Mmmmm!!

If there is a particular song or songs you would like to hear, you may make requests using the form below (your computer might give you a couple of warnings that you are sending an email that isn't private etc. but don't worry, you can send it safely).
